About Me

Researcher | Building Capacities

My Journey

I am Oladoyin Akintola, a researcher working at the intersection of sustainable infrastructure, decision intelligence, and climate-responsive development. I hold a Bachelor of Technology in Quantity Surveying from the Federal University of Technology, Akure, where I graduated with First Class Honours.

My academic training provided a strong foundation in construction management and economics, which I apply to understanding how the construction industry can transition toward net-zero systems.

Research

Research spans circular construction, net-zero transitions, and infrastructure sustainability. Published in leading journals including Energy and Buildings and Construction Economics and Building.

Industry Experience

Graduate research assistant with the Research Group on Sustainable Infrastructure Management Plus (RG-SIM+), while working as Project Quantity Surveyor to maintain industry alignment.

Sustainability

Actively engaged in national sustainability efforts through contributions to the Green Building Council Nigeria and Nationally Determined Contributions projects.

My Mission

My work is driven by a commitment to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), especially within the context of developing economies. I believe in the power of research, education, and advocacy to drive meaningful change in how we build and maintain our infrastructure.
